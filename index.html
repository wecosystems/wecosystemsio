Use Cases

We will increase accessibility, usability, and scalability based on the actual use case.

Fanbacker
FanBacker is a platform for fans to invest in and support promising celebrities before they become mainstream. By using NFTs, they aim to create a tradable market for these investments.
Media Content (ArenaCast)
Streaming service ArenaCast broadcast e-sports and sports games, as well as its own games. Various streamers are invited to participate through Gamification to maximize fun and scalability in eSport and New Media.
Challenger
Challengers can find a challenge that suits them by viewing various challenges in real time, and anyone can immediately try it when they are ready after checking the challenge rules.
Game (Rankingball)
RankingBall is a play-by-play strategy prediction game in North America and Europe. Rankingball quantifies the numerous real-time events that happen in a sports/eSports game and reflects them in a scoring system. Its interface is modeled after bingo, one of the most popular games around the world.
Pick & Go
Pick & Go is a game in which you complete your own bingo board after selecting your favorite team and player you support in a real-time game match.
RightsOn
In the upcoming Web3 world, all rights can be freely traded. We are conducting NFT/STO platform business in the global leisure industry such as golf, travel, and lodging. At Rights On, we are creating a world where people can own and use a wonderful resort on the Hawaiian beach, and also trade the rights to others at any time.
BIX Exchange
Our goal is to establish a global direct investment platform for bio-pharmaceutical research and development pipelines based on blockchain RWA and launch the GLOBAL IP TRADE MARKET, allowing direct investment in technology rather than companies.

    Phase 1

  • Foundation & Proof of Concept
  • Platform MVP :
    - Launch of basic RWA tokenization framework.
    - Initial NFT marketplace supporting fractional ownership.
  • Pilot Entertainment IP :
    - Tokenization of a select film or digital performance IP to prove concept viability.

    Phase 2

  • RWA Scale-Out & AI Enhancement
  • Leisure & Franchise Onboarding :
    - Tokenizing first real estate assets (e.g., hotels, resorts) and franchise businesses.
  • AI Data Integration :
    - Deploy oracles for property valuations, occupancy metrics, and social media scraping for IP analysis.
  • Initial DeFi Tools :
    - Launch staking, basic lending, and yield farming functionalities with RWA collateral.

    Phase 3

  • Comprehensive DeFi & DAO Implementation
  • Advanced DeFi Suite :
    - Introduction of derivatives, LSD, re-staking options, and insurance protocols for RWA tokens.
  • DAO Governance Rollout :
    - Transition to an on-chain governance model, enabling proposals and community votes on platform changes.
  • Strategic Partnerships :
    - Collaborations with major entertainment agencies, real estate conglomerates, and stablecoin providers.

    Phase 4

  • Global Expansion & Full Interoperability
  • Cross-Chain Bridges :
    - Official bridging solutions for RWA tokens across major blockchains (Ethereum mainnet, Polygon, BNB Chain, etc.).
  • Regulatory & Legal Frameworks :
    - Registration in multiple jurisdictions, obtaining relevant licenses/approvals.
  • More Exchange Listings :
    - Targeting top-tier centralized exchanges (Binance, Coinbase, Kraken) for WECO token listing and deeper liquidity.

    Phase 5

  • Mainstream Adoption & Beyond
  • Metaverse Integration :
    - Partnerships with AR/VR and gaming platforms, letting users explore tokenized real estate and digital celebrities in immersive 3D environments.
  • Mass Marketing Campaigns :
    - Leveraging global ambassadors, influencer marketing, high-profile events to attract tens of millions of new users.
  • Open API & Developer Ecosystem :
    - Launch of developer grants and hackathons to foster third-party tools, dApps, and expansions of WECO’s core functionalities.
